About us

In 2010 we, Phil and Jen Puzzo, decided to incorporate as Puzzo Masonry. With our loyal employees and dedication to service, we are proud to be a part of various projects within Chicagoland and its suburbs.

Certifications

OSHA 3115, OSHA 10 Hour Construction Safety, Silica 4 Hour, Fall Protection 4 Hour, Telehandler Operator Training, and the DuPont Weatherization Systems course.

Trusted

Trusted by many such as Red Seal Homes, Toll Brothers, Landmark Development, Balsitis Contracting, Airoom Architects, Builders and Remodelers, Maki Construction, RG Ventures, Lotz Custom Carpentry, and many more.

Services

We offer many services such as commercial and residential construction, restoration, renovations, remodeling, tuck pointing, fireplaces, chimney repair, mailboxes, wing walls, hardscape, outdoor kitchens, power washing, caulking, water leaks, and water proofing.
